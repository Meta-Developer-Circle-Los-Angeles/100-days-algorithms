Subject:
Given an array of size n and multiple values around which we need to left rotate the array. How to quickly print multiple left rotations?

Example:
Input : 
arr[] = {1, 3, 5, 7, 9}
k1 = 1
k2 = 3
k3 = 4
k4 = 6
Output : 
3 5 7 9 1
7 9 1 3 5
9 1 3 5 7
3 5 7 9 1

Input : 
arr[] = {1, 3, 5, 7, 9}
k1 = 14 
Output : 
9 1 3 5 7
